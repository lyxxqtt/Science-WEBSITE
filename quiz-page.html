  2. Write the letter of the correct answer.
  3. Capital letters only. No erasures.
  4. You have 1 minute to answer each question.

Question 1:

These are organisms that can consume both plant and animal material, making them highly adaptable in their dietary habits.

Results:

Your Answers:

Correct Answers:

  1. C: Omnivore (These are organisms that can consume both plant and animal material, making them highly adaptable in their dietary habits. Omni = "all")
  2. A / D (Desert ecosystems are defined by extremely low rainfall and can be either hot or cold. Antarctica is an example.)
  3. A: Earth (The biosphere is part of Earth, but not all of Earth is part of the biosphere.)
  4. D: Leave lights and appliances on when not in use. (Please don't waste electricity.)

    B. A branch of biology that examines ecosystems and the relationships within them.
    C. The science that explores how living things depend on and impact their surroundings.
